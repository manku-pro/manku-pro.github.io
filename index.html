About Klpk Church

We are helping people with the Cretor!

The Kanisa la Pentekoste Kijitonyama (KLPK) in Swahili or Kijitonyama Pentecost Church is a local church, within “The Pentecost Churches Union in Tanzania”with acronym PCUT. The Kijitonyama Pentecost Church, registered its trusties in the name of “The Registered Trustees of Kijitonyama Pentecost Church” on 15/11/1982, and then the Church was officially registered on 26/9/1983 to the Registrar of voluntary parties in the Office of Ministry of Home Affairs.
