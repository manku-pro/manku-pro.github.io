welcome to kijitonyama pentecost bible school (kpbs)

School History

The Kijitonyama Pentecost Bible School is a college that provides theology education at the level of certificate. This college was established on 07/01/2008. It has long-term experienced teachers. Course is taught for three terms within one year. The first term begins in January.

Subject undertaken

This school teaches various subjects in Swahili language for one year, for three terms. The lessons learned have helped prepare the staffs in various church services. School has enough teachers with long-term experience.

The following subjects are offered

1.An investigation of the Old Testament covenant 2.New Testament investigation 3.The foundations of the Faith 4.The Holy Spirit 5.Spiritual leadership 6.Methods for preaching Part 1 & 2 7.Mission work sections 1 & 2 8.History of the church 9.History of Israel 10.Methodology for church activities part 1 & 2 11.The life of the Christian community and the marriage 12.Charismatic and Pentecostalism 13.Psychology 14.The Constitution of the Church and the PCUT

Bible School Administration

The school is headed by the Head of School who is Bishop Methusela J. Mpera and the academic Master is Pastor Joseph Mwandembo

School Location

The school is located in the plot of Kijitonyama Pentecost church, kijitonyama mabatini street. Plot no 11,12,13 Block No 47.
