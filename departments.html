Welcome to Chirdren Department

The Children department is one of the departments in the Kijitonyama Pentecost Church (KLPK). This department deals with giving service to children in the church, at home and at school. This service is provided, to enable children have a good parental care and training on spiritual values. This gives children a sense of character and good conduct in their lives. The purpose of the children’s department is to lead children to the knowledge of salvation by the Lord Jesus. Children ministry is a very important service in the church. Obviously life begins at childhood and continues to grow physically; similarly, spiritual life begins in childhood. If the baby is well-nursed, well-fed and well-cared for, he/she grows with good health and good conduct. This can be observed in all aspects of his life, namely, the soul and the body. The time we spend teaching children, builds up the image of their future life. The Bible says, “Nurse a child in the good way; he will not leave it even when he is old” (Prov. 22: 6). In other words the thing we teach children, they live in their memory throughout their life; they cannot be forgotten besides rate age. Knowing that we have been called, to serve God and to make all people disciples of Jesus, the church directs his resources and strength into this area which have a very big return for ​​God’s work.The Lord Jesus gave the children ministry great concern and made it his priority. The Church considers today’s Children are the builder and part of the expansion of the church tomorrow. Therefore the church has a good strategy for managing the children’s department by looking at the needs of the equipment, the kind of training to their teachers and their self-sacrificing. Emphasis is on to develop teachers in order to improve the training or teaching given to the children.

Through this Department the Church receives the following benefits: – 1.The Church enables children to learn God’s knowledge well and understand them from their childhood rather than waiting while they are grown to adulthood.
2.Through teaching to the children the Church is preparing tomorrows servants who will make the church continue doing well in near future.
3.The Gospel of Jesus Christ is based on showing love to people, so it is also important to show children this unconditional love of Jesus at their tender age. Through their services children are taught this great love of Jesus.
4.The Church uses this department to build a foundation for the lives of children who are the church leaders in the future and who will carry the word of Christ for the next generation.
5.Child ministry is a way to reach others, reaching children is easy to reach parents and their neighbors. When a parent observes what is going on inside his child, even if he is far off he can do the follow-up and finally in that way get convicted.

The Children Department's structure in KPC

Unlike the Youth and Women’s Departments that are located at the Branch, Parish, and Missions levels, the Children’s Department is located at the Branch level. The future view of the Church is to introduce teacher’s organ at Parish and Mission level.Leadership The Children’s Department is headed by Sunday school Teachers. However there are branches where the children’s department is under elder who has the responsibility of directing department and coordinating the Sunday school teachers.

Welcome to Choir Department

The choir Department is one of the Church departments that are part of the Kijitonyama Pentecost Church. As it was since the Old Testament times, the Church had priests and singers (the sons of Asaph), singing songs, praise and worship is part of Worship. KLPK introduced this Department as a tool for placing together the singers so that they could learn, practice, prepare themselves and then serve the church.

The existence of the choir department the Church and choir members receives the following benefits: 1.Choir offers services through songs that builds and teaches the church members spiritually. Since all songs are composed from the word of God.
2.Choir is an integral part of all Church worship and through praise and worship; the Choir leads the Church to call God’s Presence and to prepare the way before the Preacher.
3.The choir is closely associated with the Evangelism ministry while preparing and conducting gospel crusades and participates in preaching the gospel through the songs.
4.The Choir Department puts singers together in the Church and stimulates the singing talent.

The Choir Department's structure in KPC

Unlike the Youth and Women’s Departments that exist at the Branch, Parish, and Mission levels, choir Department exist at the Branch levels only. In the branch there are more than one choir due to the growth of the relevant branchThe Church has benefited from the existing choir in the Church, although the operating system may vary from one branch to another depending on the circumstances and history of the Branch and the existing choirs


Welcome to Uinjilist Department

Huduma ya Uinjilisti ni huduma muhimu sana ya injili inayoliwezesha Kanisa kutambua na kuwaunganisha watu wote waliomo katika kanisa na jinsi ambavyo neema mbali mbali za utendaji zinavyofanya kazi katika kutimiza kazi ya injili. Kwa sababu hiyo watu waliomo katika huduma ya injili wengi wao wana wito wa uinjilisti na utendaji wa wito mara nyingi hutofautiana kutoka mtu hadi mtu.Bwana Yesu aliliagiza Kanisa kueneza injili ulimwenguni kote (Mk 16:15). Yesu alipokusudia kwenda katika miji na mahali mbali mbali, aliwatuma watu aliowachagua ili wamtangulie kwenda huko kwa kusudi la kufanya maandalizi ya safari zake za kutangaza neno la Mungu katika sehemu hizo. Aliwatuma, kati ya wale sabini aliwaweka, wawili wawili. Basi, baada ya hayo Bwana aliweka na wengine, sabini, akawatuma wawili wawili wamtangulie kwenda kila mji na kila mahali alipokusudia kwenda mwenyewe (Lk 10:1). Kwa msingi huo kazi ya injili tangu mwanzo ilipangwa na kuratibiwa ilikishirikisha wanainjili kuitenda.Kama Yesu alihitaji watu wa kumtangulia alikokusudia kwenda, KLPK inahitaji nguvu ya pamoja zaidi. Umoja wa huduma ya uinjilisti kama Yesu alivyowatuma wawili wawili, unawapa uwezo wa kutenda kazi ya Mungu kwa nguvu zaidi, kuliko kama kazi ile ingefanywa na mtu mmoja mwenye wito wa uinjilisti.

Kwa kuwepo Huduma hii, Kanisa linapata manufaa yafuatayo: 1.Huduma ya uinjilisti huratibu na kusimamia kazi ya injili kanisani kwa kuandaa mipango ya namna ya kutekeleza kazi hiyo na kisha kulihusisha Kanisa katika utekelezaji.
2.Huduma ya Uinjilisti huandaa mikutano ya injili katika matawi mbalimbali ya kanisa la KLPK.
3.Huduma ya Unjilisti hushughulika na ushuhudiaji aidha wa kundi au wa mtu mmoja mmoja.
4.Huduma ya uinjilisti hufuatilia waongofu wapya, ambao wameokoka katika mikutano waliyofanya, ili waweze kusimama katika wokovu. Hii ni muhimu sana ili kazi iliyofanyika iweze kuzaa matunda yanayodumu.
5Huduma ya Unjilisti hulea na kukuza vipawa vya wainjilisti, na kuwafanya watambulike na kutumiwa na watu wengine ndani na nje ya Kanisa.
6.Huduma ya uinjilisti inahusika na kufuatilia fedha zinazotolewa na kanisa au michango mbali mbali inayotolewa kwa ajili ya injili, na kuhakikisha kuwa fedha hizo zinatumika sawa sawa na malengo yaliyokusudiwa.
7.Viongozi wa huduma hii hufanya vikao mbali mbali vinavyohusu maendeleo ya injili katika kanisa. Pia wanao wajibu wa kukumbusha uongozi wa kanisa juu ya mipango mbali mbali ya injili iliyopo kanisani na jinsi ya kuifadhili ili iweze kutimizwa.

The Huduma ya uinjilisti Department's structure in KPC

Muundo wa Huduma ya Uinjlisti katika KLPK unanzia ngazi ya Tawi, Parishi hadi Misheni. Mtiririko ufuatao unaonyesha muundo wa huduma hii. Kwa kawaida shughuli za Huduma ya Uinjilisti katika Tawi huendana na mipango na utendaji wa kila siku wa Tawi, kwa upande wa Kituo Huduma hii inafanya mambo maalum yaliyopangwa na Viongozi wa Huduma ya Uinjilisti katika Parishi ili yafanywe kwa kupindi au siku husika.Kwa ngazi ya Misheni, Huduma ya Uinjilisti huratibu mambo yatakayofanyika Kimisheni na kushirikisha Baraza la Wazee na Wainjlisti ambapo huduma mbalimballi hufanyika ikiwa ni pamoja na Mikutano ya Injili iliyoandaliwa Kimisheni na kufanyika katika matawi. UongoziMwenyekiti, Mwenyekiti msaidizi, Katibu, Katibu msaidizi, Mhasibu na Mtunza Hazina. Pia Idara huwa na Mwalimu wa Kwaya ya Uinjilisti pale ambapo huduma hii ipo.
