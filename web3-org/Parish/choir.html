Welcome to Choir Department

The choir Department is one of the Church departments that are part of the Kijitonyama Pentecost Church. As it was since the Old Testament times, the Church had priests and singers (the sons of Asaph), singing songs, praise and worship is part of Worship. KLPK introduced this Department as a tool for placing together the singers so that they could learn, practice, prepare themselves and then serve the church.

The existence of the choir department the Church and choir members receives the following benefits: 1.Choir offers services through songs that builds and teaches the church members spiritually. Since all songs are composed from the word of God.
2.Choir is an integral part of all Church worship and through praise and worship; the Choir leads the Church to call God’s Presence and to prepare the way before the Preacher.
3.The choir is closely associated with the Evangelism ministry while preparing and conducting gospel crusades and participates in preaching the gospel through the songs.
4.The Choir Department puts singers together in the Church and stimulates the singing talent.

The Choir Department's structure in KPC

Unlike the Youth and Women’s Departments that exist at the Branch, Parish, and Mission levels, choir Department exist at the Branch levels only. In the branch there are more than one choir due to the growth of the relevant branchThe Church has benefited from the existing choir in the Church, although the operating system may vary from one branch to another depending on the circumstances and history of the Branch and the existing choirs
